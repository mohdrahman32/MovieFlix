Fun Facts

"Housefull" was one of the first Bollywood films to use the concept of multiple marriages for comedy, leading to a series of hilarious mix-ups.

"F2: Fun and Frustration" had a unique promotional gimmick where the lead actors dressed up as each other's characters for the trailer launch.

In "Geetha Govindam", the phrase 'Butta Bomma' from the song became so popular that it was used in everyday conversations in Telugu states.

"Deadpool" broke several records, including the most F-words in a Marvel movie, with 84 uses.

"Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo" featured a dance sequence where Allu Arjun performed with his real-life father, Chiranjeevi, in a cameo, creating one of the most memorable scenes of the film.
